Programs for every fighter

Whether you are stepping on the mats for the first time or sharpening competition skills, our training blocks meet you where you are.

Gi Jiu Jitsu Adult (14 +)

Designed to provide students with a solid base in Brazilian jiu-jitsu through theories, techniques, and principles. Our classes incorporate both fundamentals and drills designed to show students how to use the fluidity of movement, transitions, and offensive/defensive strategies to improve their game. You can equate these maneuvers to a form of kinetic chess where a submission hold is the equivalent of checkmate.

Kids Jiu Jitsu

Our Brazilian jiu-jitsu Youth program will help build self-confidence, respect, courage, discipline, sportsmanship, concentration, and coordination. The techniques taught will show students how to defend themselves through the use of leverage and angles. Our instructors teach effective self-defense while making each class challenging, technical and fun. Through martial arts your child will develop skills that will last him/her a lifetime.

No Gi Jiu Jitsu (14 +)

Our no gi jiu-jitsu program focuses on learning and applying the fundamentals of Brazilian jiu-jitsu without the traditional gi (kimono). Also known as “Submission Wrestling” this program is often the base for students preparing to compete in Mixed Martial Arts (MMA). Our no -gi program is a mix of BJJ, Freestyle Wrestling, Greco Roman Wrestling, as well as some Japanese styles.

Kickboxing (14 +)

Based on a Dutch style of kickboxing, our programs are highly technique based. Classes are strongly focused on establishing foundational skills and conditioning. Open to both competitive and non-competitive students, our kickboxing program is approachable at any fitness level and promises to provide a full body workout!

Randori

Free hour of rolling (real time sparring in jiu jitsu) or can be used as drill time with a training partner.
