Programs for every fighter

Whether you are stepping on the mats for the first time or sharpening competition skills, our training blocks meet you where you are.

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Gi and no-gi classes focused on technique, situational drilling, and live rolling led by experienced black belt instructor - Dru Hillyard.

  • Beginner & fundamentals classes
  • Intermediate + advanced training
  • Competition team sessions

Boxing & Striking

Develop precision striking, and conditioning with pad rounds, partner drills, and controlled sparring.

  • Bag work & pad combinations
  • Footwork and defensive drills
  • All levels welcome

Kids Martial Arts

Structured sessions that teach discipline and respect while improving coordination, focus, and self-defense awareness.

  • Little Warriors (ages 4-8)
  • Junior Champions (ages 9-14)
  • Confidence and fun first

Meet the coaching team

Our coaches combine decades of competition experience with a passion for helping members progress safely.

Coach Dru

Dru Hillyard is the gym’s founder and a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu black belt who has spent years competing and coaching across Atlantic Canada. His classes focus on building strong positional fundamentals so every student, from new white belts to seasoned competitors, can develop confident, efficient grappling.

Coach Ashley

Ashley Hillyard helps run Evolution’s day-to-day operations while leading fundamentals, women’s, and youth sessions. She blends her brown belt experience with a background in fitness instruction to create inclusive training spaces that emphasize technical detail, conditioning, and positive mat culture.

Coach Dawson

Dawson Sampson is a competition-tested brown belt who anchors the gym’s no-gi and wrestling-focused classes. Drawing on years of success at regional tournaments, he pushes athletes to connect takedowns with tight control on the ground while maintaining the welcoming atmosphere Evolution is known for.
