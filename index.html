Programs for every fighter

Whether you are stepping on the mats for the first time or sharpening competition skills, our training blocks meet you where you are.

Adults • Gi

Gi Jiu Jitsu Adult (14 +)

Build a strong Brazilian jiu-jitsu base with structured drills, positional rounds, and live sparring. Learn to blend fluid transitions with smart strategy on the mats.

  • Technical drilling with positional focus
  • Live sparring to apply new skills
  • Coaching tailored to every experience level

Youth • BJJ

Kids Jiu Jitsu

Boost confidence, discipline, and coordination through playful technique. Classes focus on practical self-defence while keeping training upbeat and supportive.

  • Confidence-building games and drills
  • Practical self-defence foundations
  • Supportive coaching for young athletes

Adults • No-Gi

No Gi Jiu Jitsu (14 +)

Level up grappling without the gi by combining BJJ, wrestling, and submission fundamentals. Ideal for MMA cross-training and athletes who like a faster pace.

  • Blend BJJ, wrestling, and submission grappling
  • Fast-paced rounds for MMA crossover training
  • Footwork and grip-fighting focus

Adults • Striking

Kickboxing (14 +)

Sharpen striking technique with Dutch-style pad work, bag drills, and conditioning. Classes welcome every fitness level and deliver a serious sweat.

  • Dutch-style pad and bag work
  • High-energy conditioning blocks
  • Open to every fitness level

Open Mat

Randori

Open mat style rolling to test new techniques or drill with teammates. Stay playful, experiment, and build mat time in a coached environment.

  • Coach-supervised sparring sessions
  • Experiment with new techniques
  • Build mat time alongside teammates
